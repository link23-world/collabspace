As we work to achieve our vision, we will hold ourselves to six key values:

  1. Open — We will promote best practices in open science in everything we do, including tool development, standards we set for genomic tools, and sharing of research.
  2. Accountable — We will be open to criticism and feedback and will have clear mechanisms for reporting these including a strict and enforced code of conduct.
  3. Respectful — We will acknowledge what we do and do not know and what we need to learn and we will respect the diversity of perspectives and experiences of those engaging with link23.
  4. Trustworthy — We will build partnerships where two-way trust is established between link23 and our community. We will listen to the needs of our community and will not overpromise.
  5. Inclusive — We will welcome people from diverse backgrounds and with multidisciplinary skills and actively remove barriers preventing diversity in members.
  6. Evolving — We will work with our community members to evolve link23 in a direction that benefits the community best.
